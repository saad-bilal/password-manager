What is Zapp?

Zapp is a password manager that makes it easy and secure to log in to your favorite websites and apps. With Zapp, you don't have to remember or type your passwords anymore. You just need to tap your phone or wearable device to zap your passwords to your browser and mobile app. Zapp uses end-to-end encryption to protect your passwords from hackers and prying eyes. You can also sync your passwords across all your devices and access them anytime, anywhere. Zapp is the ultimate password manager for the modern world.
